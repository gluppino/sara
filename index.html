Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a method of treatment by which specific points on the body are stimulated. Methods of stimulation include; pressure, needles, fluid, electrical current, and heat. The goal of acupuncture is to restore the flow of energy throughout the body and help the body to become balanced once again to restore health. Acupuncture is a very safe medical procedure and a typical session lasts between 30-60 minutes. Acupuncture has been proven to be very beneficial in reducing pain, regulating gastrointestinal motility, decreasing inflammation, stimulating the immune system, aiding in hormone regulation, and promoting micro-circulation.

Chiropractic

Chiropractic, or spinal manipulation therapy (VSMT), is a manual therapy that looks for decreased motion and restriction in the joints of the spine. These restrictions can cause a decrease in normal movement and cause negative effects to the nervous system. During a chiropractic exam the joints in the body are taken through a series of motions to identify restrictions and decreased range of motion (subluxations). An adjustment is made through a very quick and concise movement and is very safe for the patient.
